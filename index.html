The Flow

From booking to door code in five steps.

01

Guest books your property

GuestKey creates their portal automatically. Connects to your PMS or works standalone — either way, a personalized registration link goes out.

02

Every adult registers

Verified name, address, email, phone — under 2 minutes each. The main guest shares the link with everyone in their party. You don't lift a finger.

03

Deposit collected

Stripe-powered security deposit. No deposit, no door code. Collected automatically before check-in — returned automatically after checkout.

04

Everything unlocks

Door codes activate, the guidebook becomes the default view, and the noise dashboard opens. Guests control their stay from one portal.

05

Check-out → auto-cleanup

Codes expire, deposit returned, guest data archived for marketing and compliance. Ready for the next reservation.
